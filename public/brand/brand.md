# clipa — Brand Context

## Product
clipa is a screen recording + video editing app.
Target users: creators, devs, designers who record quick demos/tutorials.
Tone: playful but precise. Friendly, not corporate. Fast, not heavy.

## Logo
Soft 3D blocks (slight depth, matte finish, rounded corners).
Three color blocks arranged asymmetrically — evokes "clips" being arranged on a timeline.

## Brand colors (from logo)
- Purple:  #A855F7 → #C084FC (gradient)
- Blue:    #5B8DEF
- Pink:    #EC4899
- Background: prefer near-black or deep neutral; colors pop on dark

## Design principles
- Soft 3D / subtle depth (match logo's matte-block feel), no hard skeuomorphism
- Generous whitespace; content breathes
- Rounded corners (16–24px) consistent with logo's radius
- Motion should feel "snappy clip" — short durations (150–250ms), ease-out
- Never use pure flat material design; never use glassmorphism overload
